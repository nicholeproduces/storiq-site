What storiq does

Capture and organize

Snap a photo. AI labels your items instantly. Track items visually across bins, room, and locations.

Search and locate items

Search by key word, dictation, and see where items are stored so you can find things faster.

Anyone managing physical inventory across spaces — especially when it needs to be found, moved, or handed off.
