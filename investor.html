storiq Investor and Product Context

storiq is an AI-powered inventory and task handoff product for teams and households managing physical things across real-world spaces.

Product overview

storiq combines practical inventory management with clear task execution. Users can capture and organize items, locate them quickly, and convert selected inventory into focused handoffs.

The core model is simple: inventory is the system of record, and handoff is the action layer.

Founder and product vision

Build a calm, trusted system for people who manage physical inventory but need work to move quickly between people, locations, and moments.

Product philosophy

Task handoff system with inventory underneath: keep context where it belongs, share only what is needed, and reduce coordination friction.
